The Voice of Professional Musicians

The AFM is the largest organization in the world dedicated to representing the interests of professional musicians. Local 12 represents many of the finest professional musicians from Bakersfield to Sacramento — performing every style imaginable.

Our members include household names like Bruce Springsteen, Roberta Flack, Santana, Yo-Yo Ma, Sheila E, Willie Nelson, and Branford Marsalis — proof that Local 12 stands alongside the best in the business.

Everything You Need to Build Your Career

From contract negotiation to legal protection, Local 12 gives you the tools and backing to thrive as a professional musician.

Contract Protection

Negotiated agreements with the recording industry, motion picture studios, jingle producers, TV networks, and more.

Royalty Defense

We protect your recorded works and live performances from exploitation, ensuring you're fairly compensated.

Fair Wage Scales

Access established wage scales for every gig type — nightclubs, symphony stages, recording sessions, and touring.

Legislative Advocacy
