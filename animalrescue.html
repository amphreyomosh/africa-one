Our Story

Veterinarians & Volunteers United for Dogs

Animal Rescue Squad International is a 100% volunteer-run, donation-funded nonprofit founded by a passionate group of veterinarians and animal lovers. We rescue neglected dogs locally and internationally, then find them loving homes in the United States through extensive social training and careful placement.

All our adoptable dogs are fostered in Davis, California. They receive the very best care before joining their forever families up and down the West Coast.

The Process

How We Find Dogs Their Forever Homes

Our thorough process ensures every dog and every family is perfectly matched — creating bonds that last a lifetime.

01
Rescue

We rescue neglected and abandoned dogs locally in California and internationally, giving them a safe haven to begin their recovery.

02
Vet Care

Every dog receives comprehensive veterinary care — health checks, vaccinations, spaying/neutering, deworming, and microchipping.

03
Foster Home

Dogs are placed with loving foster families in Davis, CA where they receive social training to prepare for their new life.

04
Perfect Match

We carefully match each dog with the ideal family, ensuring personality, lifestyle, and needs align for a lifelong bond.

🏥 Our Commitment

Every Dog Arrives Healthy & Ready

Before any dog goes to their new home, they receive comprehensive veterinary care from our expert team of specialists and surgeons.

Fully Vaccinated

Up-to-date with all necessary vaccines and preventative treatments before adoption.

Spayed / Neutered

All dogs are spayed or neutered before placement, contributing to responsible pet ownership.

Dewormed

Fully dewormed and treated for parasites so your new companion starts fresh.

Microchipped

Every dog is microchipped for permanent identification and peace of mind.

Social Training

Each dog receives individualized social training to ensure they're the perfect fit for their new family.

Vet Specialist Team

Founded by internal medicine specialists and surgeons — your dog's health is in expert hands.
