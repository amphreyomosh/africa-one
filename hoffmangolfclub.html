Why Join AHGC

A Private Club
Experience

The Ancil Hoffman Golf Club offers membership that makes you feel like you are at a private club, with a packed calendar of competitions, a transparent Tournament Index system, and a genuinely welcoming community.

31 tournaments per year with both Gross and Net flights

Large fields of 100+ players — active, competitive membership

Tournament Index system — no handicap manipulation ("bagging")

4 quarterly member meetings with full dinner and drinks

NCGA discounts, special offers, and host course for the Memorial Amateur
